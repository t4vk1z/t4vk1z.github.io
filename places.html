PLACES

  • La Sagrada Familia

    La Sagrada Familia, also known as the unfinished masterpiece of Antoni Gaudí, is the maximum exponent of Catalan Modernism.

  • Casa Batlló

    This jewel of Catalan Modernism, is located on Gràcia avenue. It's known due to its spectacular facade inspired in the organic shaped of nature.

  • Les Rambles

    This 1.5km-long avenue goes from Catalunya square to Colombus statue.

  • Parc Güell

    Declared Humanity Patrimonym is one of the most beautiful parks over the world and another essential spot to see in Barcelona.

  • Casa Milà

    Walking above Casa Batlló on Gracia avenue, you'll find Casa Milà, another magnificient Gaudí work. This Catalan Modernism building was built from 1906 to 1912. It's also known as La Pedrera.

  • El Mercat de la Boqueria

    Located in Les Rambles, La Boqueria market is the most famous market in Barcelona. It's a 2500Km2-long enclosure with more than 300 stalls.
